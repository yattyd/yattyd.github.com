Radio Basics: Receiver
lThe RF Transceiver contains both Receiver and Transmitter.
lThe receiver selects the desired signal and removes all other signals present at the antenna (strong interferers will be present both in and out of the ISM band).
lThe desired signal is downconverted to baseband and demodulated.
lA digital bit stream representing the demodulated data is output to the baseband sub-system for further processing.
